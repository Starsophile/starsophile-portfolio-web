About Me

I'm a 19-year-old B.Tech CSE student at ITMVU with a deep passion for the intersection of technology and user experience. My journey is driven by an insatiable curiosity about how products shape our digital lives.

From analyzing the latest smartphone innovations to exploring the transformative potential of AI, I thrive on understanding what makes products tick. I don't just use technology—I dissect it, question it, and envision how it can be better.

As a Content Writer & Research Analyst at GadgetsTurbo, I've honed my ability to break down complex tech concepts into compelling narratives. My mission? To bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and meaningful user experiences, creating products that don't just work—they inspire.
